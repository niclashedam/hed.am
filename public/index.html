A few paragraphs about me

With over a decade of technical expertise and a solid commitment to security and privacy, I focus on ensuring critical infrastructure’s confidentiality, integrity, and availability. Driven by a passion for creating a safer digital society, I am committed to defending data against emerging threats and safeguarding privacy in an increasingly complex digital landscape.
